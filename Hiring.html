Custom Tech enterprise is an innovative company that focuses on crypto/blockchain technology and liberty. Our goal is to give our contractors a working environment that provides them with the best lifestyle possible  

1. Freedom of Movement - CTE contractors can preform their work anywhere in the world, all you need is an internet connection. So feel free to travel!  

2. PAX-GOLD (PAXG) - Payment is provided in a stable-coin crypto currency that is pinned to the value of gold, the oldest most stable form of currency in the world. PAXG does not inflate like fiat currency (USD for example) nor does it swing wildly in value like other non-stable cryptocurrencies. It is easily exchangeable for US dollars, or the currency of your choice. You can even use your PAXG to make online purchases through a debit card from Unbanked.  

3. Flexible Hours - Teams will work together remotely to complete projects, so you can work together to set your own hours.  

4. Production Pay - Contractors are paid upon completion of each project, no waiting on money until payday, the faster you work, the faster you are paid. This is a job that favors motivated individuals.  

5. Opportunity for Advancement - Contractors who successfully complete jobs will have the opportunity to create and manage their own development teams.  

6. Add to Your Skillset - Additional training can be provided.  

Developer  

Software Developers at Custom Tech Enterprize (CTE) are the backbone of our initiatives to design, code, test, and provide cutting-edge solutions that pave the path for the future, like blockchain and cloud technologies, cryptocurrency, and various other fields of software and hardware. Whether you are working on projects internally or for a client, software development is critical to the success of CTE and our clients worldwide. At CTE, you will use the latest software development tools, and work with clients to build solutions you can be proud of.

This role requires a self-starter with strong technical and problem-solving skills, the ability to collaborate and effectively communicate with cross functional and cross geographical teams, and knowledge and application of blockchain and cloud technologies. The ideal candidate for this position will have an established background in blockchain and cloud native software development, agile methodologies, and Dev-Ops practices. Candidates should be motivated and have passion to continuously acquire new skills and achieve results in a client outcome-focused environment.

 

Required Technical and Professional Expertise  



• Experience in or ability to quickly acquire and master skills in Blockchain and cloud technologies, IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), Web3 standard protocols, HTML/CSS, and other software and hardware items

• Demonstrate a high degree of experience in general blockchain and cloud native software development and debug with JavaScript, HTML/CSS, JSON and GitHub/source code management


 

Salesperson  

As a sales contractor for Custom Tech Enterprise your job will be to convince prospective clients to chose CTE for their development contracts. The primary tool you will use for finding clients is Gitcoin, a blockchain online development bounty board built ontop of Github. Development skills aren’t required, but you must know enough about the company to sell our services. You will need to be part of the development ecosystem, and we will train you on the use of Github, Gitcoin, and all other relevant tools.

 

In addition, each sales contractor will be responsible for creating and managing a social media account for the company. You will use BlackMagic studio to create advertisement material  

It is our opinion that closed-source software is not sustainable, and we are committed to using open-source software at every possible opportunity. We are an ethical company and would like you to represent CTE truthfully and positively. The Ideal applicant for this job is someone who is personable, polite, and comfortable talking with people.
